The page is a Wikipedia article about _The Drama_, a 2026 American romantic comedy-drama film written and directed by Kristoffer Borgli. It stars Zendaya and Robert Pattinson as Emma Harwood and Charlie Thompson, an engaged couple whose relationship unravels after Emma reveals a traumatic and disturbing teenage secret related to a planned school shooting and her hearing loss.

The article covers the film’s plot in detail, including the couple’s wedding preparations, interpersonal fallout among friends and coworkers, and the tense events leading up to the wedding reception and aftermath. It also outlines the cast, production history, costume design, filming locations, music, release and marketing details, box office performance, critical reception, and controversy over the film’s depiction of school violence.
