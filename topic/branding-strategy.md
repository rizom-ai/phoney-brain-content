title: Branding Strategy
---
Sam Shao’s profile centers on branding as both a professional discipline and a strategic lens. She runs The Fourth, a branding and business development agency that helps companies clarify who they are, who they are speaking to, and how they should be positioned in the market. The focus is on identity, audience targeting, messaging, and differentiation rather than surface-level polish. Branding here is treated as business infrastructure: a practical way to make a company legible, distinctive, and aligned with its customers. This is the core professional topic anchoring her work and writing.
